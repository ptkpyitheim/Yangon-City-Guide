    Religion and Culture

  • Shwedagon Pagoda
      • 326 ft. tall glided golden stupa. Most sacred Buddhist pagoda in Myanmar.
  • Sule Pagoda
      • Stupa in the heart of downtown. Rallying point in both 1988 uprisings and 2007 Saffron Revolution.

    Food

  • Lucky Seven
      • Classic tea shop in multiple locations. Good for 3 pm work break.
  • Feel
      • Modern yet traditional and authentic Burmese cuisine
  • Chinatown
      • Vibrant nightlife. Thrify shopping. Various grocery choices. Best streetfood location.

    Activities

  • Chin Lone
      • Individuals kick-passing the handwoven rattan ball (sounds like a basket when hit) in a circle.
  • Thingyan
      • Burmese New Year celebrated in Mid-April. A.K.A Water Festival.
